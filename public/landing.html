Frequently Asked Questions.

Quick answers to common questions about our platform, security, and integration.

How does predictive scaling work?

Our engine analyzes 50+ historical traffic patterns and signal markers using LSTM neural networks. It preemptively triggers capacity adjustments 5-10 minutes before an anticipated surge hits your fleet, ensuring zero-latency scaling and maximum reliability.

How secure is the AWS integration?

Security is our core priority. We use cross-account IAM roles with granular, least-privilege permission sets (read-only for metrics, auto-scaling for cluster management). We never store long-term secrets or your AWS root keys.

Do you support other cloud providers?

Currently, the CloudPulse AI Engine operates natively with AWS EC2 and Auto-Scaling Groups. However, our architecture is cloud-agnostic, and multi-cloud readiness for Azure and GCP is currently in closed-beta for Enterprise clients.

Can I use this with existing EKS clusters?

Yes. While we specialize in EC2 Auto-Scaling, our predictive agent can integrate with Kubernetes Horizontal Pod Autoscalers (HPA) to provide the same intelligence layer to your containerized workloads.
